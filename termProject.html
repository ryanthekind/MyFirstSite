“We Will Not Be Silent”

John Hancock employees wearing the 2018 PROUD t-shirt

PROUD is a grassroots organization that aims to promote an inclusive workplace for lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender (LGBT) employees in order to promote their full and unencumbered contributions to Manulife/John Hancock.

Our Mission:

  • Promote a positive work environment for LGBT employees and enhance their engagement and retention.
  • Promote cultural awareness and diversity of thought internally
  • Provide professional development and networking opportunities for LGBT employees and their “allies”, including employees working remotely
  • Provide informal mentorship through the sharing of experiences
  • Support people who want to be comfortable with being “out” at work
  • Support the Manulife/John Hancock brand by developing a credible presence within the local community
